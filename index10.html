               Eligibility:-

✇ Must have a Bachelor’s Degree in any discipline from a recognized university. Must be a citizen of India. Can apply up to the Certain Eligible Age which is specified by the Gujarat Police Department in recruitment notification.

               Age:-

✇ Minimum: 21 Years, Maximum: 35 Years. category-wise age relaxations applicable. Male:- Hieght=162 cm, Chest=79 cm(Unexpandable) 84(Expanded) Weight=20Kg, Female:- Height=156 cm Weight=40 kg
