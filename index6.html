♕ What to study to become a doctor ?

✇ After MBBS (Bachelor of Medicine, Bachelor of Surgery) you can become a doctor. This is the only course through which you can become a doctor. And after completion of M.B.B.S you can do its master degree M.S (Master of Surgery) and M.D (Doctor of Medicine)

♕ Which entrance exam should you give ?

✇ For admission one has to take the NEET (National Eligibility cum Entrance Test) exam. Which is collected every year by CBSE. Then you can take admission in your favorite college

♕ what are the qualifications required ?

✇ 10+2 with science strem. subject:- physics, chemisty, bioligy

♕ what is the age limit ?

✇ Must be at least 17 years old and maximum 25 years old when applying for MBBS.
